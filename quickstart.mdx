## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">
- A modern web browser: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (version `>120`)
- A valid email address for account verification
- Team member emails ready for invitations
</Callout>

Pavel Melnik works entirely in your browser at `https://dashboard.example.com`. No downloads required.

## Create Your Account and Log In

Follow these steps to sign up and access your dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="globe">
    Open `https://dashboard.example.com` in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up** and enter your email, full name, and a secure password.
    
    Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard, enter your credentials, and click **Log In**.
    
    You land on the personalized dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configure Initial Dashboard

Customize your workspace for efficient project management.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set Workspace Name" icon="settings">
    Click the profile icon in the top-right and select **Workspace Settings**.
    
    Enter a name like "Engineering Team" and save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Theme" icon="sun">
    In settings, select **Appearance** and pick light or dark mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Notification Preferences" icon="bell">
    Under **Notifications**, enable email alerts for task assignments and deadlines.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create and Organize Your First Project

Launch a project to track tasks and milestones.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Project" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Project**.
    
    Name it "Q4 Product Launch" and set a description: "Coordinate marketing and dev for holiday release."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Boards" icon="layout">
    Inside the project, create boards: **Backlog**, **In Progress**, **Review**, **Done**.
    
    Drag sample tasks between boards to test workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Milestones" icon="flag">
    Add milestones like "Design Complete" (target: 2024-11-15) and "Beta Release" (2024-12-01).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invite and Onboard Team Members

Add your team to collaborate seamlessly.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email Invites" icon="mail">
    In project settings, go to **Team** > **Invite Members**.
    
    Enter emails: `team@company.com`, `dev@example.com`.
    
    Set roles: **Admin**, **Editor**, or **Viewer**.
    
    They receive an email with a join link.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Shareable Link" icon="link">
    Select **Generate Invite Link** for quick sharing via Slack or chat.
    
    Customize expiry: `7 days` and max uses: `10`.
    
    Team members click the link to join instantly.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  After inviting, assign initial tasks to new members from the **Backlog** board for smooth onboarding.
</Callout>
