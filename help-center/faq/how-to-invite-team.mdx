---
title: How do I invite my team?
description: Guide for inviting teammates to your Pavel Melnik workspace.
---

## Steps

1. Open your Project Dashboard.
2. Click "Invite Team" in the sidebar.
3. Enter email addresses of team members.
4. Select access level for each.
5. Click "Send Invites".

## Notes

- Invited users get an email with a join link.
- Track pending invitations under "Team Members".
